The department of Computer Science (CS) at the University of Montana offers an undergraduate bachelor of science (BS) degree and a graduate level masters degree (MS). We also have a minor in CS. Most of our courses are delivered by full-time faculty members and instructors. Our department prides itself on having faculty who are highly accessible and dedicated to our students, who teach and conduct research in a wide variety of different topic areas, and who have developed strong research programs that provide new opportunities to our students. Teaching assistantships and research assistantships are available to some of our graduate applicants. Please look around our site to get to know us a little bit better and don't hesitate to contact us with any questions you may have. Welcome again!
